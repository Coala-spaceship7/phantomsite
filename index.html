👻 10 Most Haunted Places in the World

1. Tower of London – England

Site of executions and imprisonments for centuries, visitors report sightings of the ghost of Anne Boleyn and other former prisoners.

2. Poveglia Island – Italy

Once a quarantine station and later a mental asylum, this island near Venice is said to be haunted by thousands who died there.

3. Aokigahara Forest – Japan

Known as the "Suicide Forest," it's said the spirits of those who died there wander among the dense trees.

4. Bhangarh Fort – India

Considered one of the most haunted places in India, local legend says a curse was placed on the fort centuries ago.

5. Château de Brissac – France

Home to the "Green Lady," a ghost said to be a noblewoman murdered here in the 15th century.

6. Eastern State Penitentiary – USA

This abandoned Philadelphia prison is famous for reports of ghostly figures and eerie sounds in its crumbling cellblocks.

7. Hoia Baciu Forest – Romania

Nicknamed the "Bermuda Triangle of Romania," visitors report strange lights, unexplained illnesses, and apparitions.

8. Chernobyl – Ukraine

The abandoned city of Pripyat near the nuclear disaster site is often described as a ghost town frozen in time.

9. Island of the Dolls – Mexico

Hundreds of decaying dolls hang from trees on this Xochimilco island, said to honor a girl who drowned nearby.

10. Edinburgh Vaults – Scotland

These underground chambers beneath the city are known for reports of ghostly encounters and unexplained cold spots.
